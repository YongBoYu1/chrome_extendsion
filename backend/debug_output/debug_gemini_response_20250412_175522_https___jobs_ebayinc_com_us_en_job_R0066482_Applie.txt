## Job Overview: Applied Researcher 2 at eBay Toronto

This document describes the Applied Researcher 2 position within the Engineering department at eBay, located in Toronto, Canada. The role is hybrid and focuses on enhancing the seller and buyer experience through advanced algorithms and machine learning. The position involves designing, developing, and deploying machine learning models to improve personalization, recommendation systems, messaging, reviews, and trust-building on the eBay platform.

## Responsibilities and Contributions

The Applied Researcher 2 will lead the design and implementation of algorithms to enhance the selling experience and build user trust. This includes developing machine learning models and scalable data pipelines to deliver data-driven solutions. The role requires staying updated on the latest advancements in algorithms and machine learning, integrating innovative techniques to improve business metrics and user experience. Collaboration with cross-functional teams is essential to transition research insights into production systems, ensuring scalability and performance. The researcher will also conduct experiments and analyze data to drive continuous model improvements and communicate research findings to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

## Qualifications and Skills

Candidates should possess an MS or PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Data Science, or a related field, or equivalent experience. A minimum of 3-5 years of industry experience with an MS or 1-3 years with a PhD in designing, developing, and deploying machine learning models is required. Strong expertise in machine learning and data science, with experience in areas such as Generative AI, NLP, recommendation systems, ranking, fraud detection, or online advertising is crucial. Proficiency in Python, Java, or similar languages, with experience using ML frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and familiarity with big data tools such as Hadoop, Spark, and SQL are also necessary. Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment are essential. A proven track record of publishing research in top-tier conferences or journals and experience in e-commerce or a related industry are considered a plus.
